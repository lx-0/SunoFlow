## What this does

Human-shareable links: the chosen slug IS the shareToken (global unique, 409
on collision with a friendly message). Lifetime is enforced derived — no
cron: reads treat past-expiry sessions as closed, the prompt push rejects
them up front AND inside the conditional-increment budget gate (no race past
the deadline). Accepted trade-off (recorded in M005-CONTEXT): human slugs
are guessable; damage is bounded by budget cap, per-guest limits, veto, and
the lifetime itself.
