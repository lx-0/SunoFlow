## What this does

**Pass 1 -- Audit-Constraints (was Code/Infra darf nicht brechen):**

1. **GlobalPlayer-Slot** -- persistent bottom of AppShell. Konstrukt-Critical.
2. **Race-Guards in GlobalPlayer** -- 7 useRef (load-generation token, peaks-worker, etc.). Refactor-Constraint.
3. **AUDIO_CACHE survives deploys** -- `sunoflow-audio-v2` stable across builds, user-saved offline songs.
4. **Per-deploy cache-busting** -- BUILD_ID-driven SW namespacing. Deploy-Constraint.
5. **Generation pipeline integrity** -- 4-file canonical-flow (`api/generate` → `lib/sunoapi` → `lib/songs/lifecycle` → `api/webhooks/suno`). Nicht zerstoeren.

**Pass 2 -- IA-Constraints (was URLs/Layouts darf nicht aendern):**

6. **Public-URLs unveraendert** -- `/s/`, `/p/`, `/u/`, `/embed/*`. External consumers (geteilte Bookmarks).
7. **`/admin/*` Sub-App** -- separates Layout (AdminShell), eigene 22 admin-API routes. NICHT in main IA.
8. **`/api/v1/*` programmatic** -- API-Key-auth, externe Konsumenten. NICHT in main IA.
9. **Auth-Flows separate Routes** -- `/login`, `/register`, `/verify-email`, `/forgot-password`, `/reset-password`. Nicht in Tab-Hubs verstecken.
10. **AppShell-Sidebar-Pattern (`hidden md:flex md:fixed md:inset-y-0`)** -- Desktop-Sidebar bleibt; nur Items reduzieren.

**Pass 3 -- Responsiveness-Constraints:**

11. **md: (768px) ist Desktop-Pivot** -- kein neuer Breakpoint einfuehren (296 tailwind-classes wuerden brechen).
12. **Mobile-First Default** -- 51% sm:, 38% md:, 11% lg+. IA-Redesign muss thumb-reach bleiben.
13. **44px Touch-Target Minimum** -- AppShell-nav-items haben `min-h-[44px]`. Mobile-Constraint.
14. **`pointer: coarse` runtime check** -- 2 surfaces (LibraryView, swipable-song-row) entscheiden swipe-vs-click zur Runtime. Mobile-Pattern beibehalten.

**Pass 4 -- PWA-Constraints:**

15. **Standalone PWA-Display** -- `manifest.json` `"display": "standalone"`. ServiceWorkerRegistrar + per-deploy cache-busting.
16. **Audio-Aware Auto-Reload** -- SW reload-prompt wartet wenn `isAudioPlaying()`. Mobile-PWA-Constraint.

**Pass 5 -- Persona-Constraints (aus USER-JOURNEY §1):**

17. **Single-tenant** -- pro User eigene Suno+OpenAI-Keys, eigene Credits. Kein Multi-User-Catalog.
18. **Mobile-Power-User Persona** -- thumb-reach, on-the-go workflow, generation-loop-zentral.

**Pass 6 -- Hot-Files Blast-Radius Warnings:**

19. **god-objects respektieren** -- 6 Components mit >30 commits seit Jan. IA-Aenderungen die diese touchen sind eigene Engineering-Projekte, nicht "Nav-Tweaks". Concrete: LibraryView, AppShell, SongDetailView, GenerateForm, GlobalPlayer, QueueContext.

Pass 7 -- Out-of-Scope Reminder:
